Music for Belcastro

Station playlists for in-house music at Belcastro.

What are they for?

These playlists are used by Barix Exstreamer 120's for different zones in the house.

What do I care?

You probably don't. But - if you want to consume them - do so as below.

How do I use them?

Add the playlist reference to one of your sources, then update and reboot. Don't change the $NUM part - that lets you change stations. The playlist is:

http://music.belcastro.net/playlists/playlist$NUM$.m3u
